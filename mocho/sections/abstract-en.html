Abstract

***MOCHO***, or **M**id-level **O**ntology for Cross-domain **C**ultural **H**eritage **O**bjects, is a reusable alignment layer between the Europeana Data Model (EDM) [1] and GLAM-spanning domain ontologies. MOCHO defines WEMI-typed entities and four alignment patterns. WEMI stands for Work, Expression, Manifestation, Item, and is a conceptual model that first appeared in FRBR for describing bibliographic resources [2].
